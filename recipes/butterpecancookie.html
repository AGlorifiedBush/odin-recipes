Brwon Butter Pecan Cookies

These we're my grandmas favorire cookies. She used to make them every morning before we would go to Six Flags.

Fun for the whole family to make memories and bite in to the sweet, delicious morsels pf flavor that just sing to your taste-buds!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cups of Chopped Pecans
  • 1 Cup of Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Cup of Firmly Packed Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup of White Sugar
  • 1 Large Egg, at Room Temperature
  • 1 Large Egg Yolk
  • 2 Teaspoons of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 1/2 Cups of Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon of Baking Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Baking Soda
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Ground Nutmeg

Steps

  1. Roast pecans in a large skillet over medium heat for about 5 minutes then set aside in a small bowl to cool
  2. Wipe the skillet and add butter put the heat on medium-low until butter begins to brown, then place in a large mixing bowl and allow to cool for 20 minutes
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper
  4. Add brwon sugar and white sugar to mixing bowl and mix with butter till combined; then add egg, egg yolk, and vanilla. Mix until well-combined, then mix in baking soda, salt, baking powder, nutmeg, and half of the flour
  5. Once combined add in the rest of the flour, then stir in the roasted pecans
  6. Scoop 3 tablespoons of dougha nd form into a ball, repeat for the remainder of the dough
  7. place dough balls on the baking sheet, about 1 1/2 inches apart and flatten out to about 1/2 inch thick with a glass
  8. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes when they just begin to brown. Allow cookies to cool for a few miutes before eating
  9. Enjoy!
