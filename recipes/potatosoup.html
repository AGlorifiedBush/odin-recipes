Baked Potato Soup

A cold day can be brightened up by a warm hearty bowl of my favorite Potato Soup. This recipe is perfect for a winter evening or just a good lunch for the next day.

This simple recipe only using 10 iongredient can carry you so far in the kithcen. If your cooking to serve family, spite loved ones, or show your angry Mother in Law that you really do more than drink beer and watch TV; then this is the recipe for you!

Ingredients

  • 12 Slices of Bacon
  • 2/3 Cup of Butter
  • 2/3 Cup of Flour
  • 7 Cups of Milk
  • 4 Large Baked Potatoes
  • 4 Green Onions, chopped
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon of Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 1/4 Cups of Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Cup of Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon in a large skillet, let sit on a paper towl to drain then break into crumbs
  2. Melt the butter in a pot, then slowly mix in flour, whisk until well-combined
  3. Then carefully pour in milk and continue to whisk until smooth
  4. Next you want to add your potatoes and onions, bring the pot to a boil stirring frequently
  5. Once brought to a boil reduce heat to a simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Lastly stir in bacon crumbs, cheese, sour cream, and seasonings; continue to cook until the cheese has melted
  7. Serve in a bowl and enjoy!
