Pan-Seared Tilapia

This is a quick and simple recipe that anyone can enjoy. This recipe has some suggested seasoning but you have a lot more freedom of choice in this one.

Tilapia is a rather tasty and inexpensive fish, it takes on whatever you season or couple the dish with, so a vegetable like broccoli can go very far if seasoned correctly and paired with this meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 Tilapia Filets
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 Cup of Flour
  • 1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil
  • 2 Tablespons of Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon of Lemon Juice
  • 1 Teaspoon of Chopped Parsley
  • 1/2 Teaspoom of Chopped Thyme

Steps

  1. Rinse tilapia filets in cold water and pat dry, season both sides with salt and pepper
  2. Put flour in a shallow dish and gently press each filet into the flour and cout both sides, shake off any excess
  3. Heat olive oil on medium-high heat in a large skillet, cook tilapia, cook in batches if needed
  4. Brush melted butter onto filets in the last minute before removing from heat
  5. Drizzle filets in the lemon juice and garnish with parsley and thyme
  6. Enjoy!
